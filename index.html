Getting Started
When you first open Mindkraft, a short tour walks you through the core idea. You then choose: Quick Start — creates 3 ready-made activities and 2 challenges — or Build My Own to start from scratch. Either way, you can edit or delete everything later. Your progress saves to the cloud every time you complete an activity.
Dimensions → Paths → Activities
Everything is organised in three layers. Dimensions are big life areas (e.g. Health, Career). Inside each, Paths are specific approaches (e.g. Morning Fitness). Inside each Path, Activities are the concrete daily actions that earn XP (e.g. Run 5km). Create these in My Activities using the + button.
Completing Activities & Earning XP
The My Activities tab shows what's due today. Tap any card to mark it done — XP floats up as confirmation. A small undo button appears if you tap by mistake. Long-press a card to edit, delete, view history, or configure it. Activity slots unlock as you level up: L1 = 4, growing quickly.
Frequency Types
Daily — resets every day, builds streaks. Occasional — any day, no streak pressure; can auto-delete after completion. Weekly / Bi-weekly / Monthly — periodic windows with streaks. Custom — set a day interval or pick specific weekdays.
Card Types & Views
Toggle between List and Grid view on the My Activities header. In Grid mode, long-press any card to choose from 7 card layouts — including a full-width feature row. Your layout choices are saved automatically.
Daily Planner
Open the Daily Planner from the My Activities header to schedule activities at specific times. Tap + Add, pick any activity or write a note, toggle Recurring to repeat daily. Completing a planned item awards XP just like the main list.
Streaks, Shields & Bonus XP
Consistent completions build your streak. From streak 5 onward you earn exponential bonus XP — streak 10 ≈ 4×, streak 30 ≈ 17× base XP. Streak Shields (up to 3) absorb missed days so one slip doesn't reset everything. Shields regenerate over time.
Levelling & Dimensions
You have a global level plus a separate level per Dimension. Levelling speed uses the formula round(k × (2L − 1)) — adjust k in Level Scaling. Dimension levels unlock cosmetic rewards. Negative XP can drain within a level but can never de-level you.
Challenges
Personal Challenges are time-boxed goals with bonus XP on completion. Group Challenges let a group each nominate a personal challenge to contribute — a shared leaderboard tracks everyone's progress. Completed challenges can be taken again.
Friends & Social
Add friends in the Friends tab using their MK-XXXX code (find yours in the Add Friend section). View their level, spider chart, and top activities. Share your progress with a Share Card on level up.
Spider Chart & Life Balance
Your Profile shows a spider chart across five categories: Body, Mind, People, Work, Extra. Go to Profile → Configure Chart to assign activities to categories. The chart updates live and shows on friends' profile cards.
Analytics
The Analytics tab shows XP over time. Use Cumulative vs Daily views, overlay any dimension/path/activity with Compare, and toggle Hide Overall to compare overlays directly. Below: Activity Calendar, full History, XP & streak leaderboards, frequency charts, dimension progress.
Themes
Pick your look in Settings — a focused Dark mode or a clean, airy Light mode. Hit Apply Theme to persist. Custom theming — every colour, glow, and gradient under your control — is coming soon.
Backup & Data
Mindkraft auto-creates a cloud backup every time you save. Hit Backup Now for an immediate snapshot. Use Restore Backup to roll back. Export a full JSON file and Import it on any device. Works across all your devices.
Rewards & Profile
Tap your avatar to open Profile — set a username, view stats, and define Rewards tied to level milestones. Rewards also unlock on Dimension levels. Your character title evolves based on your most-invested category. A special surprise awaits at Level 100.
Negative Activities & Skip Penalty
Activities can award negative XP for habits you want to break. The Skip Penalty option auto-deducts XP the next time you open the app after missing a day — great for hard accountability. Penalties appear clearly in your Activity History.

How activities work

Activities, XP & Streaks

Base XP
Pick how hard the habit is. Easy for low-effort actions (drink water), Medium for daily disciplines (workout), Hard for serious effort (deep work). XP grows with your streak.
Frequency
How often the activity should appear. Daily shows every day; Custom lets you set "every 3 days" or "Mon/Wed/Fri". Occasional is for one-offs.
Streaks
Every consecutive completion grows your streak. Streaks add a multiplier to your XP — consistency pays compound interest.
Advanced setup
Pick a Dimension & Path to organise it, or leave it as Uncategorized. Allow multiple completions per day, or set negative XP for bad habits you want to break.

Start with one or two activities. You can always edit or delete them later.
