Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

1. Who we are

Mindkraft ("we", "us") is a life-gamification app available at mindkraft.life. It lets you track habits and activities, earn XP, build streaks, take on challenges, and share progress with friends. This policy explains what information Mindkraft collects, how it is used, and the controls you have over it.

The short version: we collect only what the app needs to work, we don't run ads or third-party trackers, and we never sell your data.

2. Information we collect

Account information

Mindkraft uses Sign in with Google (via Firebase Authentication). When you sign in we receive your Google account's name, email address, profile photo, and a unique account identifier. You can optionally set a separate username inside the app.

Content you create

Everything you build in the app is stored against your account: dimensions, paths, activities, completions, XP and levels, streaks, challenges, planner items, rewards, notes, chart configuration, and settings (including your theme). Daily cloud backups and any JSON exports you trigger contain the same data.

Social features

If you use the Friends features, your friend code, display name, profile photo, level, XP, streaks, and spider chart become visible to friends you add and to members of leaderboards and group challenges you join.

Push notification data

If you enable the daily reminder, we store your chosen reminder time and your browser's push subscription (an endpoint URL and encryption keys) so we can deliver the notification. Disabling the reminder deletes the subscription.

Automatically collected information

Mindkraft has no ads, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking pixels. Our infrastructure providers (GitHub Pages for hosting, Google Firebase for authentication and database) may log standard request metadata such as IP address and browser type as part of operating their services.

3. How we use your information

  • To run the app — storing and syncing your activities, XP, streaks, and settings across devices.
  • To power social features — showing your profile to friends you've added and populating shared leaderboards.
  • To send reminders — delivering the daily push notification you configured.
  • To generate your Tech Tree — see section 4.
  • To keep your data safe — creating daily cloud backups you can restore.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the features described above.

4. AI features (Tech Tree)

When you request a Tech Tree, the names and structure of your dimensions, paths, activities, and related progress numbers are sent to Groq, Inc., an AI inference provider, to generate the tree. Your name, email, and account identifier are not included in these requests. Tech Tree generation only happens when you explicitly request it.

5. Where your data lives & who we share it with

We share data only with the service providers required to operate Mindkraft:

  • Google Firebase (authentication and Cloud Firestore database) — stores your account and app data. See Firebase's privacy documentation.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the app's static files. See GitHub's privacy statement.
  • Groq, Inc. — processes Tech Tree generation requests. See Groq's privacy policy.

Beyond these providers, your data is shared only with people you choose to connect with in the app (friends, leaderboards, group challenges). We never sell your personal information, and we disclose it otherwise only if required by law.

A copy of your app data is also cached locally on your device so Mindkraft can load quickly and work offline.

6. Your controls

  • Export — download a full JSON copy of your data anytime from Settings → Data.
  • Reset — permanently erase all your app data from Settings → Data → Reset All Data.
  • Notifications — enable or disable the daily reminder in Settings; disabling removes your push subscription.
  • Social — you choose whether to add friends or join leaderboards; skipping these keeps your progress private to your account.
  • Account deletion — contact us (section 10) and we will delete your account and associated data.

7. Data retention & security

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. Cloud backups keep only the latest daily snapshot. When you reset your data or ask us to delete your account, the corresponding records are removed from our database.

All traffic between your device and our services is encrypted with HTTPS, and database access is restricted by per-user security rules. No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we design Mindkraft to hold the minimum data it needs.

8. Children

Mindkraft is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Material changes will be highlighted in the app. Continuing to use Mindkraft after a change means you accept the updated policy.
